Cooking is my hobby and my passion. I have always loved being in the kitchen, experimenting with different
ingredients and techniques to create delicious and satisfying meals. I find cooking to be therapeutic and
relaxing, and it brings me a great sense of fulfillment and enjoyment.

Cooking involves important life skills, particularly patience, problem-solving, and creativity. As children
wait for pasta to boil or for their chicken to cook, they will learn that they need to be patient to achieve
the results that they want.
